Need help to use the website?

Select a topic from the dropdown for instructions.

Login

  1. Enter Credentials
  2. Press Login button

Login-Forgot Password

  1. Click the forget password link.
  2. Enter your email, position, and staff ID.
  3. Click enter.
  4. Now an email will be sent to you with a reset link to reset your password.

About Us-Introduction

  1. Click edit.
  2. Make your changes in the text box.
  3. Click ok when finished.

About Us-Center Info

  1. Click edit.
  2. Make your changes in the text boxes.
  3. Click ok when finished.

Schedule-Announcment

View Details

  1. Click the details button.
  2. A pop up window will appear with all the details for the announcment.

Edit Announcments

  1. Click the edit button.
  2. Edit the announcment title and the announcment itself.
  3. Click save.

Delete Announcments

  1. Click the delete button.
  2. Click ok when the alert appears asking if you are sure you want to delete the announcment.

Add Announcments

  1. Click the green plus button.
  2. Enter in the announcment title and the announcment itself.
  3. Click submit.

Schedule-Center Schedule

Add New Schedule

  1. Click the add new schedule button.
  2. Click the drop down arrow for the week of text box and click on the date you want.
  3. Fill out the rest of the text boxes with the times you want
  4. Click submit.

Edit Schedules

  1. Click the edit button.
  2. Edit the week of text box and the other text boxes.
  3. Click submit.

Delete Schedules

  1. Click the delete button.
  2. Click ok when the alert appears asking if you are sure you want to delete the schedule.

Schedule-Working Schedule

Edit Schedules

  1. From the top bar select working schedule.
  2. Click the edit button on the schedule you wish to edit.
  3. In the pop up window click the text box according to the day you wish to change
  4. When you are finished click the submit button and the window will close.
  5. You will be prompted with an alert asking if you are sure that you want the edit that schedule, click yes

Memo

Add Memo

  1. Click the add memo button.
  2. Click the drop down arrow to enter the memos date.
  3. Enter the content of that memo.
  4. Click submit.

Edit

  1. Click the edit button.
  2. Edit the content.
  3. Click submit.

Delete

  1. Click the delete button.
  2. Click ok when the alert appears asking are you sure you want to delete this memo.

Status Record-Daily Status

  1. From the top bar select daily status.
  2. Select the date of status under date by clicking the drop down arrow and selecting a date from the calendar.
  3. Click the drop down button under patient to select the patient you wish to see.
  4. Click the display button.
  5. Now you will see the patient daily status information to your right.

Library

Filter

  1. Click the drop down to select the category of task you want to appear.
  2. Click the search bar and enter in the category or the task name you want to appear.

Details

  1. Click the details button.
  2. A pop up window will appear with all the details for the task.
  3. Click the here button for more details.

Request Copy

  1. Check the boxes next to the tasks you want a copy of.
  2. Click the request copy button.
  3. Your copy will then appear under my tasks
