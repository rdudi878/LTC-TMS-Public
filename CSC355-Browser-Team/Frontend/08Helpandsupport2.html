Schedule-Center Schedule

Add New Schedule

  1. Click the add new schedule button.
  2. Click the drop down arrow for the week of text box and click on the date you want.
  3. Fill out the rest of the text boxes with the times you want
  4. Click submit.

Edit Schedules

  1. Click the edit button.
  2. Edit the week of text box and the other text boxes.
  3. Click submit.

Delete Schedules

  1. Click the delete button.
  2. Click ok when the alert appears asking if you are sure you want to delete the schedule.

Schedule-Working Schedule

Edit Schedules

  1. From the top bar select working schedule.
  2. Click the edit button on the schedule you wish to edit.
  3. In the pop up window click the text box according to the day you wish to change
  4. When you are finished click the submit button and the window will close.
  5. You will be prompted with an alert asking if you are sure that you want the edit that schedule, click yes

Events

Add an event

  1. Click the plus google calendar button in the bottom right corner.
  2. The google calendar website will appear on another browser tab and you will then click the create button with a plus in the top left corner.
  3. A small window will appear and you will then enter in all the information for the event.
  4. If you need more event options when entering in your event information, click the more options button at the bottom of the small window.
  5. When finished, click save and close the google calendar website.

Events

Print an event

  1. Click the print icon on the top right corner.
  2. Enter in your printing configuration.
  3. Click print.

Events

Edit an event

  1. Click the plus google calendar button in the bottom right corner.
  2. The google calendar website will appear on another browser tab and you will then click an event you would like to edit.
  3. A small window will appear and you will click the pencil icon at the top center of the small window.
  4. Enter in your changes to the event.
  5. When finished, click save and close the google calendar website.

Events

Delete an event

  1. Click on the event from the calendar and click more details.
  2. The google calendar website will appear on another browser tab and you will then click an event you would like to delete.
  3. A small window will appear and you will click the trash can icon at the top center of the small window. This will delete the event.
  4. When finished, close the google calendar website

Events

Email an event

  1. Click on the event from the calendar and click more details.
  2. The google calendar website will appear on another browser tab and you will then click an event you would like to email.
  3. A small window will appear and you will click the email icon at the top center of the small window.
  4. Another small window will appear and you will then enter in the email of the person you would like to send the event to and a message that you may want to add as well.
  5. When finished, click send and then close the google calendar website.

Events

Change view of calendar

  1. To change the view of the calendar go to the top bar.
  2. On the left of that top bar you can change the month you are viewing with the arrow buttons, go back to the current day by pressing the today button, and select a specific day by selecting a day of a month and year from the dropdown.
  3. On the right of that top bar you can change the calendar to a month or week view by clicking the respective buttons that say month and week. You can also view the calendars agenda by clicking the agenda button.

Events

More details of events

  1. Click on the event from the calendar and click more details.

Events

Copy events to calendar

  1. Click on the event from the calendar and click copy to my calendar.
  2. The google calendar website will appear on another browser tab and you will need to enter in all the appropriate information.
  3. When finished, click save.
